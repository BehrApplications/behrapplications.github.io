Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Behr Applications, our apps, and how we work.

FAQ List

We build applications from the user's perspective — literally. Our apps are created by people who needed them and couldn't find anything that truly worked. This means we skip the feature bloat, focus on what actually matters, and deliver software that respects your intelligence and your time. We prioritize quality over speed and privacy over data extraction.

Yes! Our aviation applications, including Fly Invoicing, are built by people who fly. We understand the specific challenges of managing invoicing in the aviation industry — the need for offline capability, the importance of professional formatting, and the unique billing structures that general-purpose tools never quite handle correctly.

Privacy is fundamental to our philosophy, not an afterthought. We collect only what is strictly necessary for the app to function, we don't sell your data to third parties, and we store as little as possible on external servers. Each app has a dedicated privacy policy detailing exactly what data is collected and why. We believe your data belongs to you.

We prioritize quality over speed. We don't release features until they're genuinely ready. We call this "sympathetic development" — we put ourselves in the user's position before writing a single line of code, and we iterate slowly and thoughtfully rather than rushing to ship. Our users deserve software that's been properly thought through.

We love hearing from users! Feedback is the most valuable thing you can give us. Reach out directly at behrapplications@gmail.com or use the contact form. Every message is read and considered seriously.

Currently our apps are available on iOS as beta releases. We're actively exploring additional platforms — including Android and web — based on user demand. If you'd like to see support for a specific platform, let us know via our contact form. User demand directly shapes our roadmap.

We update our apps regularly, but always based on user feedback and quality considerations — never just to show activity. We believe in quality over frequency. When we do release an update, it has been thoroughly tested and genuinely improves the experience. You can expect meaningful updates, not superficial ones.

Yes! We maintain an active internal roadmap shaped by user feedback. We don't publish it publicly to avoid creating expectations around timelines we may need to adjust (quality first), but subscribing to our newsletter is the best way to hear about upcoming features and releases before anyone else.
