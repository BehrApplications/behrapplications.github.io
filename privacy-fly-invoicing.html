Fly Invoicing

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14/3/2026

Fly Invoicing provides invoicing, trip tracking, and expense logging tools for freelance pilots.

Information We Collect

Fly Invoicing stores information you enter into the app, which may include:

  • Pilot details, including name, email address, phone number, license number, bank name, account name, IBAN, SWIFT code, invoice due terms, and base currency
  • Operator details, including company name, contact name, billing email address, phone number, billing address, rates, and aircraft tail numbers
  • Trip and invoice details, including trip dates, invoice numbers, notes, payment status, generated PDF invoices, and totals
  • Expense details, including expense names, dates, amounts, currencies, converted values, receipt images, and receipt PDF files

How We Use Information

We use this information only to provide app functionality, including:

  • Creating and managing pilot, operator, trip, and expense records
  • Generating PDF invoices and receipt summaries
  • Preparing invoice emails
  • Converting expense amounts into the selected default currency
  • Syncing data across your devices when iCloud / CloudKit is enabled

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not use your data for advertising or tracking.

Data may be transmitted only when required to provide app features, such as:

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit services for sync
  • Apple Mail services when you choose to send an invoice
  • Apple photo, camera, and file access when you choose to attach receipts
  • Exchange-rate services used for currency conversion

Data Retention

Your data remains stored until you delete it, remove it through the app, or use the app’s data removal options.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Edit or delete pilot, operator, trip, and expense records
  • Remove stored receipts
  • Delete pilot information
  • Delete operator information
  • Delete expense history
  • Use Eject All to remove all stored app data

Security

We rely on Apple platform security and system networking protections to help protect your data. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Fly Invoicing is not directed to children.
