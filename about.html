🌿 Our Story

About Behr Applications

We build software from a place of genuine understanding — because we've been in your shoes.

"At Behr Applications, we believe software should serve people, not the other way around." — The Behr Applications Team
🧑‍💻
Our Approach

Built by Users, For Users

Every application we create starts with a problem we've personally experienced. We don't build software in a vacuum — we build it because we needed it and couldn't find something that truly worked.

This means our design process begins with deep user research and, more importantly, personal experience. We understand the workflows, the frustrations, and the small details that make software genuinely useful versus merely functional.

🎯
✈️
Aviation Roots

Pilots by Pilots

Our aviation applications — like Fly Invoicing — are developed by pilots, for pilots. We know what it means to manage invoicing between flights, to need offline capability at 30,000 feet, and to require professional templates that match the standards of the aviation industry.

This first-hand experience is irreplaceable. It informs every design decision, every feature priority, and every interface choice we make.

🛩️
🤝
Our Philosophy

Sympathetic Development

Sympathetic development means putting ourselves in the user's position before writing a single line of code. We ask: what is the user actually trying to accomplish? What gets in their way? What would genuinely delight them?

We iterate slowly and thoughtfully, releasing only when something is truly ready. Our users deserve software that respects their time and intelligence.
