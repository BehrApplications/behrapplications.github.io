About Behr Applications

Our Philosophy

At Behr Applications, we believe in user-centric design. Our products are built by users for users, ensuring that the needs and experiences of our customers shape our development process.

For our aviation apps, we take pride in the fact that they are piloted by pilots, ensuring that every feature is intuitive and practical. This sympathetic development approach allows us to craft tools that genuinely assist those in the aviation field.

Core Values

  • User-Centric Design
  • Sympathetic Development
  • Innovation and Quality
  • Community Engagement
