Privacy Policy

G6000 Recurrent Practice — by Behr Applications

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Behr Applications ("we," "our," or "us") operates the G6000 Recurrent Practice mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at behrapplications@gmail.com.

Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when using the App, including:

  • Practice Session Data: Records of your practice sessions, scores, timing, and progression that you create within the App.
  • User Preferences: Settings and configuration choices you make within the App to personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data: Basic anonymous analytics about how you interact with the App, such as features used and session duration. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version, collected solely for compatibility and debugging purposes.

We do not collect financial information, government identification numbers, precise geolocation data, or any data that is not necessary for the App's core functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App's practice tracking and analytics features.
  • Display your practice history and progress analytics as requested by you.
  • Improve and optimize the App based on aggregated, anonymous usage patterns.
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and provide customer support.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your practice data is stored locally on your device by default. Any data transmitted to our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable law. Practice data you create in the App is stored locally on your device and is under your full control. You may delete this data at any time by removing the App from your device or using the in-app data management tools.

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 24 months to inform product improvements, after which it is permanently deleted.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your personal data.
  • Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at behrapplications@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may use limited third-party services solely for technical functionality (e.g., crash reporting). These third parties are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes beyond what is necessary to provide the service. We do not integrate advertising networks or social media trackers.

8. Children's Privacy

G6000 Recurrent Practice is intended for professional use by adults engaged in aviation recurrent training. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and, where appropriate, via in-app notification or email. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Behr Applications
Email: behrapplications@gmail.com
